    Book: The wee free men #

    The Wee Free Men are very little men who like “to fight, to drink and to drink and fight” (well, so they say…!). Well, but the main character is Tiffany, at the beginning of the book she slais Jenny green-teeth with a friyng pan (for Jenny and all other fairies iron is very painful because iron is made by humans, not by nature - well, thats the short explanation), which seems to be very impressive to other witches. in the book Tiffany is a witch, - at the beginning of the story she doesn’t know that - and she even gets a um.. real? Witch hat. Little hint: there are four books, so nothing bad should happen there…

    I like the book because it’s sometimes really, really exciting, but also REALLY funny ;)
